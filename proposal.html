PC Builder

Problem Description

The topic of our application is personal computer (PC) building. Given the wide variety of PC components available, each type of component comes in numerous variations, featuring distinct specifications and is uniquely manufactured by various brand. This wide array of components and the incompatibility of many parts makes PC building a confusing process for both experienced and inexperienced PC builders. Yes, you can watch hundreds of tutorials and eventually learn about each component, brand, their compatibility, specifications, and how to put together a PC, but that takes hours and memorization. To avoid this confusion and extra effort, many people go with pre-built PC route. This is where a manufacturer builds a complete PC that is ready for consumers to purchase. However, this route has its drawbacks. Pre-built PC's often come with a higher pricetag (compared to a self-built PC with similar components), poorer quality components, possibly lower performance, and possibly lack of customization and upgradability.
